Playground is an innovative app designed for teachers of 5â€“6-year-old students. It allows teachers to create interactive boards with various play corners and activities. Students can drag and drop their avatars to select an activity and begin playing or learning!

With Playground, the classroom becomes a dynamic space for creativity, collaboration, and hands-on learning.

How It Works

Playground makes classroom management and engagement simple. Here's how:

  • Teachers create boards with activities and play corners.
  • Each student gets a unique avatar created by the teacher.
  • During class, students drag their avatars to the desired activity to start participating.
